Meet the Designer

Lain Taylor

they/she

Lain Taylor (born December 2001 in Richardson, Texas) is a musician, programmer and amateur fashion designer based in the DFW area.
Lain is known for fusing music with clothing by putting many of the covers of their favorite albums on T-shirts, creating a means for her and others to share the music they love in their daily lives.
